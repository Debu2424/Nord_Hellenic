Scandinavia

Welcome to Scandinavia!

Scandinavia is a term that covers the countries of Denmark, Norway, Sweden, Finland and Iceland. We will be covering just Norway, Sweden and Denmark.The capital of Norway is Oslo, the capital is Sweden is Stockholm and the capital of Denmark is Copenhagen.

Places to visit in:

  1. OSLO


    Vigeland Sculpture Park:

    World's largest sculpture park made by a single artist, and comprises of Gustav Vigeland's lifework with more than 200 sculptures in bronze, granite and wrought iron.


    Akershus Fortress:

    If you would like to explore Oslo’s history the Akershus Fortress is the best place to visit! There you can see the building of Akershus Castle and Fortress that commenced in 1299 under king Håkon V.


    The Viking Ship Museum:

    For Viking Lovers this museum exhibits ships that were ocean going vessels before they were hauled onto land to be used in burial rituals for their wealthy owners. Some of the most famous ones being The Oseberg Viking Ship, The Gokstad Viking Ship, and the Tune Viking Ship.


    Nordmarka:

    A vast and beautiful area of untouched nature that surround Oslo are just 20-30 minutes from the city centre. It is  a source of recreation that is frequently used by the city's inhabitants, but also more and more visitors, both in summer and winter.


  2. STOCKHOLM


    Gamla Stan:

    The Old Town, is one of the largest and best preserved medieval city centers in Europe, and one of the foremost attractions in Stockholm. This is where Stockholm was founded in 1252.


    Vasa Museum:

    A maritime museum displaying the only almost fully intact 17th century ship that has ever been salvaged, the 64-gun warship Vasa that sank on her maiden voyage in 1628.


    The Royal Palaces:

    Is the official residence of His Majesty the King of Sweden. The Palace was largely built during the eighteenth century in the Italian Baroque style. It contains the Armory, with royal costumes and armor, as well as coronation carriages and magnificent coaches from the Royal Stable.
